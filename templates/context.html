MOTIVATION

AI, personality and Ethics

With the emergence of Large Language Models (LLMS), many studies have already been conducted on their behavior when facing ethical dilemmas. However, the link between personality and moral values had not yet been used to evaluate a LLM behavior - that's when I come in !

Linked to several subjects

Cognitive science

This research could tell if AI agents could in the future be used as participants in psychology and cognitive science research in general.

AI Development

Giving us insights on how personality simulation mignt affect moral reasoning in AI systems.

Ethics

In a world where everybody is stressed out by the rise of evil AI robots, it could help find ways to give AI moral values and making it more ethical.

OBJECTIVE

My research objectives and their evolution during the semester

At the beginning I was quite disorganized. My research englobed many question: How well can a LLM replicate a human personality? Do individual personaity traits impact differenly the morality of an AI agent? How well each individual AI agent imitate the answers of its human counterpart? These question were very interesting and I did explore them but I decided to organize the results in sub-studies I will add to my project in time.

So my unique research question is:

Can Large Language Models, when given human personality traits, reproduce their morality reasoning bias?
